Over 55 Years Experience

Established in 1964, Heaton Sanitation Ltd. has been serving Windsor, Essex County and Chatham-Kent for well over 55 years in the field of septic and liquid waste removal. Septic & Liquid Waster Removal

Our Services

With a fleet of top of the line equipment and over 55 years of industry experience behind us, its safe to say that we're experts in the sanitation business. Our services include grease trap, septic and holding tank cleaning as well as septic tank inspections. We're also Ministry of Environment approved to safely remove and transfer a wide variety of liquid waste materials to approved off-site locations.

Whether its regularly scheduled services or an emergency, our team is ready 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. That lends itself to part of the reason that Heaton Sanitation has and continues to be a trusted name in Windsor, Essex County and Chatham-Kent for over 55 years.

A Name You Can Trust

Our History

Heaton Sanitation Ltd. was established in 1964 by William Heaton. At the time Heaton Sanitation specialized in septic and liquid waste removal. In 1995 Heaton Sanitation Ltd. entered into the hydro excavation business and purchased its first Vactor Flusher unit. In 2015 Hurricane Hydrovac Services acquired Heaton Sanitation. The combined enterprises of Heaton Sanitation, Hurricane Hydrovac, and Hurricane SMS now operate a fleet of over 35 trucks and offer a variety of services including disposal, sewer and catch basin cleaning, video inspection, and hydro excavation services.
